# Audio-Agent.md (#42)

## Tagline
Procedural, executable skills for real-time voice agents.

## What It Does
Moving SKILL.md beyond text manipulation, this format defines vocal tone, interruption handling, emotional resonance, and pacing for open-source voice models. Developers can copy-paste a Late Night Radio Host or Strict Teacher voice persona instantly.

## Inspired By
VibeVoice, Whisper, Multimodal + Voice interfaces

## Viral Potential
Voice AI is the undisputed frontier of consumer interaction. Highly shareable audio demos go instantly viral on TikTok and X. Plugs directly into trending open-source models like VibeVoice.

## Unique Defensible Moat
A proprietary markup language maps semantic text tokens directly to latent audio waveform modifiers, enabling zero-latency emotional shifts during real-time generation.

## Repo Starter Structure
/parser, /audio-bindings, MIT License, local voice chat demo
